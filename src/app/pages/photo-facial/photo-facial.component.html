Photo Facial

No matter how much we take care to reduce the signs of ageing, stubborn brown spots can be a give-away. Some know these by different names - age spots, freckles, lentigines, or liver spots. There are a range of different types of pigmentation which vary in appearance, what depth the skin they lie, and how responsive they are to treatment.
Benefits:

  • Improve skin tone
  • Reduce rosacea and thread facial vein
  • Reduce facial wrinkles and fine lines
  • Treat sun damage including age spots and brown pigmentation
  • Firming and tightening the skin
  • Stimulate new collagen and elastin tissue production
